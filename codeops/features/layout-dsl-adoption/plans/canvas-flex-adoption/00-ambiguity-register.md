| AR-6 | The two `this.layout` sites on `class DetailScreen extends Group` (`router-demo:59`, `drill-down:29`). | **Preserved as assignments.** `this` already exists when the constructor runs, so no builder can produce it; expressing a merge would need the `setLayout(partial)` primitive that is #117's subject. Their children still convert. Not a defect, a boundary. | ✅ Resolved |
